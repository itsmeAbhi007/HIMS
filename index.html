FAQ
How do I register for NEET PG 2025 counseling for HIMS?

Register on the HNBUMU portal (www.hnbumu.ac.in) when the counseling schedule is announced. Enter NEET PG details, pay the fee, and check eligibility.

How does choice filling work in HNBUMU counseling?

Log in to the HNBUMU portal, select courses (MD/MS) and colleges (e.g., HIMS), and choose All India Quota or State Quota (if eligible). Lock choices before the deadline.

What happens after seat allotment at HIMS?

Pay the Advance Tuition Fee on the HNBUMU portal to get an allotment letter and Advance Tuition Fee receipt. Report to HIMS with documents within the given period.

What documents are needed for HIMS admission?

Bring HNBUMU allotment letter, advance tuition fee receipt, NEET PG 2025 Admit Card, NEET PG 2025 Scorecard, Internship Completion Certificate, MBBS degree, MBBS All Professionals Marksheets, medical registration, recent character certificate, migration certificate, one set photocopy of all original documents, Aadhaar card, PAN card, and category/domicile certificate (if applicable).

Where can I find updates on NEET PG 2025 counseling for HIMS?

Visit www.hnbumu.ac.in for NEET PG 2025 counseling schedules and updates.

Is hostel accommodation available for PG students at HIMS?

Hostel accommodation is available. It is not mandatory to stay in the hostel, but students living off-campus should reside nearby to ensure clinical duties and responsibilities are not affected.
